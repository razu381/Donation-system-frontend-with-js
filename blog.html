Important FAQ

How do I make a donation on the website?

To make a donation, simply navigate to the "Donate" page on our website. Select the cause or program you'd like to support, choose your donation amount, and fill in your personal details. We offer several payment options, including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Once your payment is processed, you will receive an email confirmation and a receipt for your records. If you encounter any issues during the donation process, feel free to reach out to our support team for assistance.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, in most cases, your donation may be tax-deductible. We are a registered [501(c)(3)] nonprofit organization (or relevant legal status in your country), meaning that donations are typically eligible for tax deductions. After completing your donation, you will receive a confirmation email that includes a receipt. This receipt can be used when filing your taxes to claim the deduction. For more detailed information, we recommend consulting with your tax advisor to confirm how the deduction applies based on your location and donation amount.

Can I donate anonymously?

Absolutely! We understand that some donors prefer to remain anonymous. During the donation process, you will have the option to select "Donate Anonymously." This means your name and personal information will not be displayed publicly or shared with third parties. However, even if you donate anonymously, you will still receive a confirmation email and tax receipt for your donation (if applicable). Rest assured, we take your privacy very seriously and ensure all personal information is handled securely.

How is my donation used?

We believe in full transparency and want you to know exactly how your donation is making a difference. Depending on the cause or program you choose to support, your donation may go towards funding specific projects, such as providing clean water, education, or emergency relief. We allocate funds based on the most pressing needs, ensuring that your donation has the greatest possible impact. You can also check our annual reports and financial statements on our website, which detail how funds are distributed and used to achieve our mission.

Can I set up a recurring donation?

Yes, you can! Setting up a recurring donation allows you to support our cause on an ongoing basis without needing to manually donate each time. When making a donation, simply select the "Recurring Donation" option and choose the frequency (monthly, quarterly, yearly) that works best for you. Recurring donations provide us with a steady stream of support, helping us plan and sustain long-term projects. You can adjust or cancel your recurring donation at any time by logging into your account or contacting our donor support team.
